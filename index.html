SAVE Secure Intake Suite

Application Assessment Portal

One locked multiple-choice assessment with monitored fullscreen, incident logging, server-side grading, and an admin review panel for submissions and violations.

Candidate Session

Locked-in testing with a real incident trail.

This build is designed around a 45-question application made up of 30 auto-scored policy questions and 15 typed written responses. Question content loads from Supabase, the objective section is scored server-side, and the written section is preserved for manual supervisor review.

45 Questions Fullscreen Required 1 Focus Warning Copy or Paste = Immediate Lock
Focus Monitoring Leaving the tab or exiting fullscreen warns once. A second focus violation locks the assessment and records it in the incident log.
Clipboard Blocking Copy, cut, and paste attempts are blocked during the live session and immediately terminate the assessment.
Admin Review Supervisors can sign in through Supabase Auth to review scores, incidents, typed written responses, and the full submission record from one panel.

Normal browsers cannot be made perfectly impossible to leave. This page uses the strongest practical browser-side controls available and then locks and logs when users break them.
